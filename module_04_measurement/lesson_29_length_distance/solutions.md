**61. Order from smallest to largest: 3 m, 300 mm, 30 cm, 0.3 km**

- 3 m = 3 m
- 300 mm = 0.3 m
- 30 cm = 0.3 m
- 0.3 km = 300 m

Answer: **300 mm, 30 cm, 3 m, 0.3 km**
Or: **0.3 m, 0.3 m, 3 m, 300 m**

**72. Classroom is 8 m long and 6 m wide. What's the perimeter in cm?**

Solution:
```
Perimeter = 2(length + width)
         = 2(8 + 6)
         = 2(14)
         = 28 m

Convert to cm: 28 Ã— 100 = 2800 cm

Answer: 2800 centimeters
```
