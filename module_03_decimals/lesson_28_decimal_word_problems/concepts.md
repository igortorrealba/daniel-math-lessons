### Types of Word Problems

#### Addition Problems
Adding decimals using real-world scenarios:
- Combining amounts of money
- Finding total distances
- Putting measurements together

#### Subtraction Problems
Removing or finding differences:
- Making change from money
- Finding how much more is needed
- Comparing measurements

#### Multi-Step Problems
Problems that need more than one operation:
- Buy something and get change
- Measure, add, then subtract
- Combine and compare

### Estimation Strategies

#### Rounding to Whole Numbers
- Round 4.7 ‚Üí 5
- Round 3.2 ‚Üí 3
- Round 5.5 ‚Üí 6 (round up from 5)

**Why estimate?**
- Check if your answer makes sense
- Find approximate amounts quickly
- Plan for budgeting

#### Rounding to Tenths
- Round 2.47 ‚Üí 2.5
- Round 3.24 ‚Üí 3.2
- Round 5.55 ‚Üí 5.6

### Problem-Solving Steps

1. **Read** the problem carefully
2. **Understand** what you're looking for
3. **Identify** which operation to use (+, -, √ó, √∑)
4. **Estimate** your answer
5. **Solve** the problem
6. **Check** if your answer makes sense

## Key Vocabulary

- **Word problem**: A math story that asks a question
- **Estimate**: A reasonable guess about the answer
- **Rounding**: Changing a number to the nearest whole number or tenth
- **Multi-step**: Using more than one operation to solve
