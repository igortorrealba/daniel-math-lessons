# Lesson 28: Solutions and Answers

## Section 1: Money Addition Problems

1. **Lucas has $4.50. His mom gives him $3.25. How much money does he have now?**

   Answer: **$7.75**

   Calculation:
   ```
   $4.50 + $3.25 = $7.75
   ```

2. **A notebook costs $2.75. A pen costs $1.50. What is the total cost?**

   Answer: **$4.25**

   ```
   $2.75 + $1.50 = $4.25
   ```

3. **Sarah found $5.00 in coins. Later she found $2.75 more. How much did she find altogether?**

   Answer: **$7.75**

   ```
   $5.00 + $2.75 = $7.75
   ```

4. **Daniel's lunch cost $6.50 at home. His snack cost $2.25. What was the total cost?**

   Answer: **$8.75**

   ```
   $6.50 + $2.25 = $8.75
   ```

5. **A toy car costs $8.99. A toy train costs $6.50. How much do both toys cost together?**

   Answer: **$15.49**

## Section 3: Measurement Problems

11. **A piece of string is 3.5 meters long. Another piece is 2.3 meters long. What is the total length?**

    Answer: **5.8 meters**

    ```
    3.5 m + 2.3 m = 5.8 m
    ```

12. **A rope measures 8.7 meters. You cut off 3.2 meters. How much rope is left?**

    Answer: **5.5 meters**

    ```
    8.7 m - 3.2 m = 5.5 m
    ```

13. **The water bottle holds 1.25 liters. The pitcher holds 2.75 liters. How much more does the pitcher hold?**

    Answer: **1.5 liters**

    ```
    2.75 L - 1.25 L = 1.5 L
    ```

14. **Daniel's height is 1.45 meters. His sister is 1.62 meters tall. How much taller is his sister?**

    Answer: **0.17 meters** (or 17 cm)

    ```
    1.62 m - 1.45 m = 0.17 m
    ```

15. **A plank is 4.8 meters long. A board is 2.3 meters long. How much longer is the plank?**

    Answer: **2.5 meters**

    ```
    4.8 m - 2.3 m = 2.5 m
    ```

## Section 4: Multi-Step Problems

16. **Maria buys a sandwich for $7.50 and juice for $2.25. She pays with $20.00. How much change does she get?**

    Answer: **$10.25**

    Solution:
    ```
    Step 1: Find total spent
    $7.50 + $2.25 = $9.75

    Step 2: Find change
    $20.00 - $9.75 = $10.25
    ```

17. **Tom has $50.00. He buys a book ($12.50) and a pen ($4.75). How much money does he have left?**

    Answer: **$32.75**

    Solution:
    ```
    Step 1: Find total spent
    $12.50 + $4.75 = $17.25

    Step 2: Find money left
    $50.00 - $17.25 = $32.75
    ```

18. **At the store, you find: Notebooks $3.50, Pens $1.75. You buy 1 notebook and 1 pen. If you pay with $10.00, what's your change?**

    Answer: **$4.75**

    Solution:
    ```
    Step 1: Find total cost
    $3.50 + $1.75 = $5.25

    Step 2: Find change
    $10.00 - $5.25 = $4.75
    ```

19. **Sarah measures two ribbons: Red 2.3 m, Blue 1.8 m. She joins them but cuts off 0.5 m. How long is the final ribbon?**

    Answer: **3.6 meters**

    Solution:
    ```
    Step 1: Find total when joined
    2.3 m + 1.8 m = 4.1 m

    Step 2: Subtract the cut-off part
    4.1 m - 0.5 m = 3.6 m
    ```

20. **A bucket holds 5.5 liters. It has 2.3 liters. You add 1.5 liters more. How much space is left?**

    Answer: **1.7 liters**

    Solution:
    ```
    Step 1: Find how much water is in it
    2.3 L + 1.5 L = 3.8 L

    Step 2: Find the space left
    5.5 L - 3.8 L = 1.7 L
    ```
