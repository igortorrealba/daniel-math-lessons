## Challenge Question Solutions

**Challenge 31: If 0.5 = 50/100, what does 0.05 equal?**

Answer: **0.05 = 5/100**

Explanation:
- 0.5 has the 5 in the **tenths** place = 50/100
- 0.05 has the 5 in the **hundredths** place = 5/100
- They look similar but are VERY different! 0.5 is 10 times bigger than 0.05

**Challenge 33: Order these from smallest to largest: 0.3, 0.03, 0.33, 0.30**

Answer: **0.03 < 0.3 = 0.30 < 0.33**

Or written as: **0.03, 0.3, 0.30, 0.33**

Explanation:
- 0.03 = 3 hundredths (the smallest)
- 0.3 = 30 hundredths
- 0.30 = 30 hundredths (same as 0.3!)
- 0.33 = 33 hundredths (the largest)
