## How to Use These Materials

### For Each Lesson:

1. **Start with `concepts.md`**
   - Read the key vocabulary and definitions
   - Takes 5-10 minutes
   - Gives overview of what you'll learn

2. **Read `explanations.md`**
   - Detailed, step-by-step teaching
   - Multiple worked examples
   - Visual descriptions
   - Takes 30-45 minutes

3. **Work Through `exercises.md`**
   - 30-80 practice problems
   - Mix of problem types
   - Progressive difficulty
   - Takes 60-90 minutes
   - Try problems BEFORE checking solutions!

4. **Take the Quiz**
   - `quiz.json` file (5 questions)
   - Quick assessment (15-20 minutes)
   - Check your understanding
   - Aim for 80%+ to proceed

5. **Check `solutions.md`**
   - All answers with explanations
   - See how to solve problems
   - Learn from any mistakes
   - Use for reference

### Recommended Timeline:
- **Each lesson:** 2-3 hours
- **Module 3 (2 lessons):** 4-6 hours
- **Module 4 (3 lessons):** 6-9 hours
- **Complete program:** 10-15 hours over 4-6 weeks

## Quick Reference Tables

### Decimal Conversions
- 1/10 = 0.1 (one tenth)
- 1/100 = 0.01 (one hundredth)

### Length Conversions
- 1 cm = 10 mm
- 1 m = 100 cm
- 1 km = 1,000 m

### Weight/Volume Conversions
